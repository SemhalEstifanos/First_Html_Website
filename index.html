My first week at AkiraChix

Introduction

My first week at AkiraChix was quite exciting and different. It was the very first time i was completley away from home. During my first week,I got to meet alot of nice people and some i still haven't had the chance to meet yet.I learned building a routine,and was getting to ready to start my 10 months journey.

Good experience

  1. The beautiful weather and environment.
  2. I got to know new people and their culture,and i even learned couple of Swahili words.
  3. I made a new friend from Rwanda.
  4. I really liked the fact that the system eases you in to reality.

Bad experience

  • As much as i like kenyan culture,i didn't like Mutake at all.
  • waking up super early was tough to get used to.
  • I forgot where i put my phone once,and worse it was on mute.

Conclusion

Even though it was a bit difficult to be away from home,my friends made things easier.The beauty of the environment,the hospitablity of Alumini,and the kindness of all the members also made it even more easy to get used to.
